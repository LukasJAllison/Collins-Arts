Fine Arts News Updates

The 2020-2021 school year has started and we are SUPER excited to have some students back in the building in 2 weeks!

This year, our department wants to focus on being a family. Like the little blue alien once learned, Ohana means family. Family means no one gets left behind or forgotten. We want to live this phillosophy this year and make sure that all of our students still feel as connected as possible.

We could not be more excited to welcome you into our Fine Arts Family (and welcome you home for some of our returning family members)!

I am excited to be the Director of the Collins Band Program. I will be teaching Percussion Ensemble, Symphonic Band Ensemble and Symphonic Band at MLCHS. Our program is one of excitement, excellence, and expectations. We are the band program of the first Female Governor and we pride ourselves on that. Our goals are to always strive for excellence and to have fun creating music. I am excited to be entering our 11th year here at MLCHS and Go Band!
