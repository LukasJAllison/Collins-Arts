Welcome to the Choir Homepage!

Choir - Jody Gravatte

I'm going into my 6th year teaching and my 2nd year at Collins High School. Growing up in Louisville, Ky, I have been involved with choir programs in school and church. I graduated from the University of Louisville with a bachelor's degree in Music Education in 2015. While there, I was a member of the world renown ensemble, The Cardinal Singers, where I traveled to Taiwan, Singapore, and South Korea. I then received a master's degree in Vocal Performance from the University of Kentucky in 2017, where I was an active member of their opera program. I grew my first choir program in Oldham County to over 250 students and the 8th grade treble ensemble was invited to the Kentucky Music Educators Association for a performance as a Distinguished Ensemble. I am always excited about the beginning of a new school year and the music that students will create. I currently reside in Louisville with my husband, Chris, and our two dogs, Blue and Roscoe.

The 2022-2023 School Year is kicking off!
