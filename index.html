Fine Arts News Updates

The 2020-2021 school year is about to start! This year, our department wants to focus on being a family. Like the little blue alien once learned, Ohana means family. Family means no one gets left behind or forgotten. We want to live this phillosophy this year and make sure that all of our students still feel as connected as possible. Because of this, we will be introducing 1 O'Clock, Fun O'Clock Fridays. Starting Friday, August 28th @ 1pm we will meet via M.S. Teams (Check out the helpful videos page for a tutorial on M.S. Teams and more) as a Fine Arts Department! The meeting is a mostly fun (very slightly informational) meeting as a chance to get to know some of your peers. There will be a theme for each, so show some pride and rock your style! The theme for the first meeting is a simple one, but a goodie: Hats/Head-Wear.

We could not be more excited to welcome you into our Fine Arts Family (and welcome you home for some of our returning family members)!

Band - Kevin Osborne

I am excited to be the Band Director of the Collins Band Program. I will be teaching Percussion Ensemble, Symphonic Band Ensemble and Symphonic Band at MLCHS. Our program is one of excitement, excellence, and expectations. We are the band program of the first Female Governor and we pride ourselves on that. Our goals are to always strive for excellence and to have fun creating music. I am excited to be entering our 11th year here at MLCHS and Go Band!
