Fine Arts News Updates

The 2022-2023 school year is about to start!

Band - Kevin Osborne

I am excited to be the Band Director of the Collins Band Program. I teach Percussion Ensemble, Symphonic Band Ensemble and Symphonic Band at MLCHS. Our program is one of excitement, excellence, and expectations. We are the band program of the first Female Governor and we pride ourselves on that. Our goals are to always strive for excellence and to have fun creating music. I am excited to be entering our 11th year here at MLCHS and Go Band!

Choir - Jody Gravatte

I'm going into my 6th year teaching and my 2nd year at Collins High School. Growing up in Louisville, Ky, I have been involved with choir programs in school and church. I graduated from the University of Louisville with a bachelor's degree in Music Education in 2015. While there, I was a member of the world renown ensemble, The Cardinal Singers, where I traveled to Taiwan, Singapore, and South Korea. I then received a master's degree in Vocal Performance from the University of Kentucky in 2017, where I was an active member of their opera program. I grew my first choir program in Oldham County to over 250 students and the 8th grade treble ensemble was invited to the Kentucky Music Educators Association for a performance as a Distinguished Ensemble. I am always excited about the beginning of a new school year and the music that students will create. I currently reside in Louisville with my husband, Chris, and our two dogs, Blue and Roscoe.

Visual Art - Jennifer Worley

Hi, my name is Ms. Worley! This is my first-year teaching art, and I am so excited to be joining Collins. I am from Louisville and went to Western Kentucky University. There I gained experience in ceramics, sculpture, painting, drawing, and graphic design. My favorite mediums are ceramics and wood-burning. Outside of creating art I love to play the guitar, drums, and sing. I can't wait to get in the classroom and meet all of you!

Visual Art & Graphic Design - Lukas Allison

This year I am teaching Art I, AP Art, Comprehensive Visual Arts, and Teaching and Learning Pathway. I'm excited to start my 4th year as a Titan! I have an open door policy and want you to come to me if you need anything (doesn't even have to be art related!). If you love a challenge, then you are going to love my classes. I keep things fun, challenging, and I try to lead you through as many real-world Art/Design projects as possible.
