Fine Arts News Updates

The 2020-2021 school year is about to start! This year, our department wants to focus on being a family. Like the little blue alien once learned, Ohana means family. Family means no one gets left behind or forgotten. We want to live this phillosophy this year and make sure that all of our students still feel as connected as possible. Because of this, we will be introducing 1 O'Clock, Fun O'Clock Fridays. Starting Friday, August 28th @ 1pm we will meet via M.S. Teams (Check out the helpful videos page for a tutorial on M.S. Teams and more) as a Fine Arts Department! The meeting is a mostly fun (very slightly informational) meeting as a chance to get to know some of your peers. There will be a theme for each, so show some pride and rock your style! The theme for the first meeting is a simple one, but a goodie: Hats/Head-Wear.

We could not be more excited to welcome you into our Fine Arts Family (and welcome you home for some of our returning family members)!

Band - Kevin Osborne

I am excited to be the Band Director of the Collins Band Program. I will be teaching Percussion Ensemble, Symphonic Band Ensemble and Symphonic Band at MLCHS. Our program is one of excitement, excellence, and expectations. We are the band program of the first Female Governor and we pride ourselves on that. Our goals are to always strive for excellence and to have fun creating music. I am excited to be entering our 11th year here at MLCHS and Go Band!

Visual Art - Matt Cockrell

I teach Art 1, Art 2, Art 3, and Art 4 here at MLCHS! In all of my classes, we advocate for ourselves, for causes and for issues through our artwork. We extend our classroom beyond the walls of the school and into the world, using digital resources. Community Art Projects, Fundraisers, Art Battles and Challenges, are just a few of the awesome things students do in my art classes. We don`t `fake art`.....we `make art`! We don`t trace or grid things out- we learn how to create by first learning the skills and the processes. I`m Proud To Be an Art Teacher at MLCHS! Go Titans!

Visual Art & Graphic Design - Lukas Allison

This year I am teaching Art I, AP Art, Intro Graphic Design, and Advanced Graphic Design. I'm excited to start my second year as a Titan! I have an open door policy and want you to come to me if you need anything (doesn't even have to be art related!). If you love a challenge, then you are going to love my classes. I keep things fun, challenging, and I try to lead you through as many real-world Art/Design projects as possible.

Media Arts - C.J. Rush

I teach Introduction to Media Arts, Video Studio Fundamentals, Studio Directing and Performance, Advanced Studio Production, Drama, as well as Collaborate with Ms. Osborne to direct and produce our plays and musicals. I am starting my 3rd full year at Collin’s and my 8th year teaching. We are given so many incredible tools in this school to make what we dream up become a reality and I am so excited to see what wonderful things the “Art’s” family creates again this year!
