Crustimony Proseedcake (erm... Customary Procedures)

I will treat you with respect so you know how to treat me. (Be Kind, Be Respectful)

Feel free to do anything you want as long as it doesn’t cause a problem for you or someone else. (Be Kind, Be Respectful, Be Responsible)

If you cause a problem, you will be asked to solve it. (Be Responsible)

If you can’t solve the problem on your own, or choose not to, I will step in and do something. (Be Safe, Be Responsible)

What I do depends on the unique individual and unique situation. (Be Kind)

If you feel something is unfair, whisper to me, “I’m not sure that’s fair.” and we will talk. (Be Respectful)

Mr. Allison's Rules for Himself

I listen to one person at a time, and I listen to students who raise their hand and wait for my attention.

I argue at 4pm. daily and on weekends/holidays.

I give timely feedback to students who turn in Timely work.

I will always help those who ask, and never force those who do not ask.

I will always do my best to help you.

I will spend most of my time helping you see what you do well… not what you do poorly.
