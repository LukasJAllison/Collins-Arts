The 2020 District Wide Art Show Was a huge success!

8 schools in the district were represented at the Wakefield Scearce Gallery. Host Matt Cockrell, (Collins Art Teacher) along with the help of Lukas Allison (Collins Art Teacher) and Kyle Gray, (West Middle Art Teacher) Planned, curated and installed over a thousand pieces of art from the Elementary and Secondary Art Classes across the District. Collins, SCHS, East Middle, West Middle, Wright, Painted Stone, Clear Creek, and Heritage each submitted work from their students. Several artists at the secondary level recieved prizes in each of four main categories. We would like to give a huge congratulations and shout out to every school, and each student who participated. We hope that this was only the first of many district wide art shows!
